# Description 
Fuse and relay mount is made to hold a Bussman 15713-24-12-22A Rear Terminal ATC Fuse Block and a GEP Power Products PDM-R3BA1, (3) Mini ISO Relay Power Distribution Module. This can support up to 200A of fuses and multiple Mini ISO relays. 

The fuse and relay box mount is made to fit the rear passenger side trunk well where the GHAS module is typically mounted on a stock car. The idea with this is you can mount the base, do all your electronics work, and then bolt the mount in (or take it out to add devices later).
