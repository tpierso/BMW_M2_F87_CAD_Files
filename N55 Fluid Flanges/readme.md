Custom ORB adapters that will mate up to the N55 engine. 

Specifically the turbo coolant outlet and the oil drain port. 

I made the coolant flange to adapt to a -06 AN ORB fitting--this file was recently updated to have provisions for the o-ring boss machine work; however, its worth noting to your machinest that the threaded area is set to the nominal diameter which means if it was machined straight up, it wouldn't have enough material to thread the 3/8-24. 

The oil drain flange does not have any o-ring boss grooves in the file; I just mentioned to my machinist to machine the o-ring boss.

Both fit to the engine perfectly; but you would want to speak to your machinist about adding the appropriate o-ring boss or machining for what fitting you plan to use.
